UTair company today

The UTair airline company is the parent of UTair Group – companies operating passenger and cargo air carriages (on planes and helicopters), as well as providing aircraft repair and technical maintenance services, personnel training, serving flights, and selling air carriages. The headquarters are located in Surgut. UTair was included in the IOSA operators registry as of 2008. The airline company's hub airport is Vnukovo (Moscow), which accommodates more than 160 UTair flights every day.
